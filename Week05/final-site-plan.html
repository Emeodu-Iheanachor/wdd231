Lagos Business Connect - Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Business Connect

This name was selected because it clearly represents a platform that connects businesses, entrepreneurs, and customers within Lagos. It emphasizes networking, growth, and local commerce.

Optional domain: lagosbusinessconnect.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as a hub for businesses in Lagos by providing business listings, networking opportunities, and membership registration. It will help users discover local businesses, connect with professionals, and access important business resources.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find trusted businesses in Lagos?
  • How can I register my business on this platform?
  • How do I contact the organization for partnership opportunities?

Color Scheme

  • Deep Blue (#0b3d91): Used for headers, navigation, and footer.
  • Gold (#f4b400): Used for accents, borders, and highlights.
  • White (#ffffff): Main background for readability.
  • Light Gray (#f1f1f1): Section backgrounds.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings (h1, h2).
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
